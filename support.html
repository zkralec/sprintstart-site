Sprint Start Support

Sprint Start helps athletes practice solo sprint starts and reaction training with randomized cue timing.

App Overview

Sprint Start is designed for athletes who want a cleaner way to train reaction and start timing without needing another person to give random start cues.

Supported Platform

Sprint Start is designed for iPhone on supported iOS versions listed on the App Store.

Common Questions

How do I restore Sprint Start Pro?

Open the app, go to Settings, and tap Restore Purchases.

Why can reaction timing vary slightly?

Reaction timing is based on the app cue. Audio route differences, including Bluetooth devices or audio latency, may slightly affect perceived timing.

How do I remove my data?

Delete the app from your device to remove locally stored app data.

What should I include when reporting a problem?

Please include your device model, iOS version, app version, and a short description of the issue.
